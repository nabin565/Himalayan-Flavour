Dal Bhat Tarkari

Dal Bhat Tarkari is the national dish of Nepal. It's a wholesome and balanced meal consisting of lentil soup (dal), steamed rice (bhat), and a vegetable curry (tarkari), often served with a side of pickle (achar) and greens (saag).

Ingredients

For the Dal (Lentil Soup):

  • 1 cup red lentils (masoor dal), rinsed
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • For tempering (jhanne): 1 tbsp ghee, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 2 dry red chilies, pinch of asafoetida (hing)

For the Bhat (Rice):

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 4 cups water

For the Tarkari (Veg Curry):

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1-inch ginger & 2 cloves garlic, paste
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Bhat (Rice): Rinse the rice until the water runs clear. Cook it in a rice cooker or a pot with 4 cups of water until fluffy and all water is absorbed.
  2. Cook the Dal (Lentils): In a pressure cooker or pot, add the rinsed lentils, water, turmeric, chopped tomato, and salt. Cook until the lentils are soft and fully broken down (about 15-20 minutes in a pot, or 2-3 whistles in a pressure cooker).
  3. Temper the Dal: In a small pan, heat the ghee. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add the dry red chilies and asafoetida. Pour this hot tempering mixture over the cooked dal and stir.
  4. Make the Tarkari (Curry): Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds. Once they sizzle, add the onion and saut√© until golden. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add the potatoes and cauliflower, and cook for 5 minutes. Add all the spice powders (turmeric, coriander, chili) and salt. Stir well.
  6. Add the chopped tomato and green peas. Cover and cook on low heat until the vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally. Add a splash of water if it gets too dry.
  7. Serve: Arrange a serving of bhat on a large plate. Pour the dal into a small bowl, and place the tarkari in another section of the plate. Serve immediately with a side of achar.
