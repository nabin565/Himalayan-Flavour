Newari Chatamari (Nepali Pizza)

Chatamari is a famous Newari snack often referred to as 'Nepali Pizza'. It's a thin, savory rice-flour crepe cooked with a delicious topping of minced meat, onions, and fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1.5 cups warm water (or as needed)
  • 250g minced chicken or buffalo meat
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the rice flour with warm water to create a smooth, thin batter, similar to a crepe batter. Season with a pinch of salt.
  2. In another bowl, prepare the topping. Mix the minced meat with the chopped onion, garlic, ginger, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat a flat non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  4. Pour a ladleful of the rice batter onto the center of the pan and quickly spread it into a thin, round crepe.
  5. Immediately spread a thin layer of the minced meat topping over the crepe.
  6. If using, you can crack an egg over the topping.
  7. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for about 5-7 minutes on low to medium heat, until the crepe is cooked and the meat is done. Do not flip.
  8. Once cooked, gently slide the Chatamari onto a plate. Serve hot with tomato chutney.
