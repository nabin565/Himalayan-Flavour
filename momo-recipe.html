Authentic Chicken Momos

These steamed dumplings are a quintessential Nepalese snack, filled with spiced minced chicken and served with a tangy dipping sauce. Follow these steps to create juicy, flavorful momos at home.

Prep Time 45 mins
Cook Time 15 mins
Servings ~30 Momos

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water (approx.)

For the Filling:

  • 500g minced chicken
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 2 tbsp melted butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough: In a bowl, mix flour and salt. Gradually add warm water and knead for 8-10 minutes until you have a smooth, medium-firm dough. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the filling: In a separate bowl, combine minced chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, cilantro, soy sauce, pepper, salt, and melted butter. Mix everything thoroughly.
  3. Shape the momos: Divide the dough into small, equal-sized balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle (about 3-4 inches in diameter). Keep the edges thinner than the center.
  4. Place a tablespoon of the filling in the center of a wrapper. Bring the edges together and pleat to enclose the filling. You can create a round pouch or a half-moon shape.
  5. Steam the momos: Lightly oil the surface of a steamer basket. Arrange the momos in the basket, ensuring they don't touch. Steam for 10-12 minutes until the wrappers are translucent and the filling is cooked through.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with a classic tomato-sesame achar (dipping sauce).
